Parties

 A gathering intended to provide the participants with fun of one sort or another. A party will usually result in damage to furniture, the building in which it is held, several people's livers, and/or someone's virginity. However, there is seldom permanent damage unless the sponsor of the party (usually called the host) serves Pan Galactic Gargle Blasters or has the lack of foresight to invite Zaphod Beeblebrox.
